Welcome to Gesture AI !

At Gesture AI, we are passionate about harnessing the power of AI and computer vision to empower individuals with physical disabilities to navigate their computers and the internet seamlessly.

Our journey began with a simple yet profound idea: to break down barriers and enable greater accessibility for all. We understand the challenges faced by those with physical limitations in using technology effectively. Through cutting-edge advancements in computer vision technology, we have developed innovative solutions that transform the way individuals interact with their devices.

Our mission is clear: to create inclusive, user-friendly experiences that enhance independence and connectivity. By leveraging AI, we aim to provide intuitive tools that adapt to the unique needs of each user, enabling effortless navigation, control, and interaction.
